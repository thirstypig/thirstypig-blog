#I love the sides, the cornbread, collard greens, cole slaw, and red potato salad.  I could have a meal with just the sides
#Parking on the street and some in the back
#Brunch, served only Fridays and Saturdays, look pretty good, with some healthy choices
#The fried chicken has a unusual dark crust from their secret spiced batter
