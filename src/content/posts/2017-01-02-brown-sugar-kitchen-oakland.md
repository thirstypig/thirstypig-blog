#Buttermilk Fried Chicken & Cornmeal Wafﬂe is a must order. Chicken is tender and juicy
#Beignets were almost too much to have at the end, but were yummy – light and fluffy
#BBQ Shrimp and grits had strong flavors. I understand the sharpness of the BBQ shrimp and sauce with the grits. I dig it
#Is super crowded on Sunday for breakfast and brunch
#Tanya Holland, chef and owner, was there. Looks as if she is quite involved with the restaurant, which is great
