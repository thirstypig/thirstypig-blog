On Thursday the 25th, Shanghai’s Food and Beverage (F&B) industry got together for a fund-raising event at Zeal, and raised more than 25k RMB for charity!

Shanghai, China. At the Bund’s newest hotspot, Zeal, the cream of the crop in the F&B industry congregated for a drink of Fizz and a few baozi in good company and to enjoy an evening of conviviality offered by the people, who know best: hoteliers!

But the event was not all about fun, as part of the ticket sales proceeds went towards the Dream Shanghai Charity ([http://www.charitydreamshanghai.com](http://www.charitydreamshanghai.com/shh-main/)), a charity dedicated to help orphaned children born with cleft lip and palate, and to provide pre- and post- surgical care to abandoned babies suffering from surgically correctable deformities.

Fizz and Baozi will be back for more early 2012 for its fourth edition.
