On December 12, 2010, I finished the last of my 100 shots at Blue Frog. On this last trip, I consumed the last portion of this epic quest – of 20 shots. But, I must confess that I failed in my attempt to finish 20 shots in a row. I could only do 16. You can check out the video below.

Blue Frog is a chain of restaurants in Shanghai, with locations in Beijing and Macao. With Western Cuisine, they feature burgers and pasta. Additionally, they also have fully stocked bars at each of their locations.

The 100 shot challenge is do-able for almost anyone. All you have to do is drink all 100 shots to complete it, with no time limit. Though you have to drink all the shots at the same location, most people usually have 5 or 10 shots per visit. Each order consists of 5 different shots – for RMB 135. You are given a card to begin your quest, to mark down your shot counts. When you complete the card (and shots), you get one shots glass, a rocks glass, and high ball glass, an umbrella, T-shirt, and cap. In addition, with every return visit to the same Blue Frog, you’ll get a free shot drink of your choice. And lastly, you get the honor of your name on the wall, with date, immortalizing your feat.

In the process of getting to 100 . . . I was able to convince 5 other friends to start their quest. 2 of them, just recently finished 100, a week after mine – Congrats to Vanessa and Felisa!

The burgers at Blue Frog are decent, but not great. I usually order the burgers and their fries. I like the fries. I don’t like their soups.

Anyways. . .when you head over to the Shanghai World Financial Center Blue Frog, look for Lily to make sure you have a good time!

What to Order: Chili Cheese Fries
What not to Order: I am not a big fan of their soups
