Nighttime views of Shanghai aren’t hard to come by, with [M1NT](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2010/12/02/the-best-damn-nightclub-in-shanghai-m1nt/), [Flair](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2011/03/16/flair-lounge-shanghai/), Vue, and Bar Rouge. But there’s a new player in town, Char Terrace at the Hotel Indigo.

This vantage point is unique in which it offers breath-taking, panty-dropping views **both** during the day and at night. The river front properties and Pearl tower on the Pudong side and the Bund views all showcase what Shanghai has to offer. I recommend getting seats right on the edge to see the buildings and structures. At night, you’ll notice the river barges floating by. Though it looks as if they are cruising blindly with hardly any lights on.

On Sunday’s there is the special afternoon Chilled Sunday promotion for RMB 99 for a mixed carafe, featuring Bloody Mary’s, Sangria, or Pimms Basic. It will run all summer from 2:30pm till 5:30pm.
