#Johnny Lee’s Hainan Chicken now in a permanent location
#Solid Effort and is getting better. May eventually dethrone Savoy.  I like the roast chicken better than the poached one
#Good location in Arcadia, with the large Chinese immigrant population
#I like the chicken soup, but I am waiting for the fried egg in chicken fat
#The Melon teas are too sweet, but after watered down from ice, they are much better
