**What is Bar Rouge**

Bar Rouge is one of the best nightclubs in the world, not just in Shanghai. Been around for years, Bar Rouge is internationally renown for it fabulous views of the Shanghai nighttime lights, the Huangpu River, and the Bund. Bar Rouge collaborates with DJ, Clubs and [Venues](http://www.venuefinder.com/) from all around the world to host some of the most memorable parties.

**What I didn’t like**
Over the years, Bar Rouge has gone through several renovations. I think as a true nightclub, Bar Rouge needs a decent dance floor. (not that I am going to cut up the rug at Bar Rouge). Bar Rouge isn’t a big place, but is a cool lounge bar. You’ll find lots of Expats and Locals.

**The Last Word**
Bar Rouge is what it is. And they do it quite well. It isn’t a place that is top of my places to go on the weekends. But it is a place to go for birthdays and for out of towners.
