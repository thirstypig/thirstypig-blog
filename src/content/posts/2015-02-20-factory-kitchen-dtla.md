#One of the best Italian dining experiences I’ve had in LA
#Could best my argument that Shanghai has better Italian than LA
#Has a subdued, yet festive space
#mandilli di seta – handkerchief pasta, ligurian almond basil pesto and porchetta – rolled pork belly, aromatic herbs, red onions, carrots, fennel, celery are awesome
#looks like it is in a shady industrial area, but appropriate for the name “factory”
