**What is the Farmhouse Caffe?**
The Farmhouse Caffe is a little restaurant located on the quaint Yongkang road. Yongkang road has a bunch of cool little eateries, cool shops, and vibrant galleries. The Farmhouse Caffe, opened by a local Shanghainese couple, is a English theme restaurant featuring British dishes inspired by Jaime Oliver.

**What did I eat?**
Miss N and I both decided on the hearty English Breakfast for RMB 48. For the price, it isn’t bad. The dish had eggs, toast, mushrooms, vegetable greens, sausage, and ham. I also got the pumpkin squash soup, which wasn’t bad.

**Constructive Criticism**
I didn’t like the sausage, but unfortunately, I yet to realize that I won’t be getting good sausage in China. Also, the soup was served lukewarm, which is bordering on disgusting. Soup NEEDS to be served pipping and almost boiling hot.

**The Last Word**
My stomach got the best of me, when I was looking for some eggs on this recent weekend. And another thing, I know that when I see eggs on the menu, I am almost certain to order it.
