#Went on a lobster roll crawl to compare some of the better lobster rolls in LA.  Apologizes to other establishments that I missed.
#EMC’s lobster roll was flavorful, creamy, and a little more refined
#Connie & Ted’s, which was the most expensive had huge chunks.  Very true to the flavor with a less saucy and less mayo roll.
#Cousin’s Maine, which probably served the most rolls, with it’s restaurant and truck, also had nicely shaped chunks
#I liked them all, but I would recommend EMC’s to a layperson.
