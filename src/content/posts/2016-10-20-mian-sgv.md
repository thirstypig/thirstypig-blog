#Red oil Chaoshou 紅油抄手, dumplings, are not to be missed
#Bean ZhaJiang Noodles 重慶豌雜面 and Crispy Minced Pork Noodles 脆臊面 are both good
#Steamed Beef Stew 粉蒸牛肉 is a classic Chinese spicy dish, but it was so-so here, and I usually love 粉蒸肉 (pork)
#Long wait during rush hours
