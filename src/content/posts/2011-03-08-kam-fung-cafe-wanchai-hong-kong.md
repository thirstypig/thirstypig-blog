One of the best desserts in the world is the simple cantonese egg tart ŤõčśíĽ or ŤõčśĆě. There are a few accounts on the origins of the mini egg tarts, found today in Chinese Bakeries & Dim Sum Restaurants. I tend to believe the Portuguese story with the tart deriving from the pastel de nata.
