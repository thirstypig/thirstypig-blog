Traditional Hong Kong style breakfast.  With the rice porridge, pineapple buns, macaroni spam in soup, rice noodle with dried shrimp, and the dry noodles. Reminiscent of my short time in Hong Kong years ago.  You can see the western British influence with these dishes.  I love the rice porridge with thousand year old egg and pork (which I didn’t get this time). The pineapple buns with the hard butter is also a favorite.  Check out my next post of the tea coffee with the condensed milk.  Too bad for breakfast they open at 9am.  They should open earlier.
