Baked Chicken at Love Baked Wings @lovebakedwings on Melrose.  Still with a nice crackle but without the greasy fried crust.  I first had baked chicken in Seoul at Oppadak @oppadak_ with a couple of local elementary school teachers.  Loved the baked concept with the dry seasonings and especially liked the dipping sauces.  Their spicy is crazy spicy!  Unfortunately their lemon rub is a bit too sour.  Read that the chef used to be @beyonce personal chef.  Would recommend and want to visit again soon.  I hope they build one in Pasadena.
