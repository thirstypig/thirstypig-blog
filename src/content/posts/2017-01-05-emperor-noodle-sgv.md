#Might be my favorite Shanghainese restaurants in LA
#[One of J.  Gold’s favorite Sheng jian Baos](http://www.latimes.com/food/dailydish/la-jonathan-golds-six-favorite-dumplings-20131104-story.html)
#Don’t care if the service is bad (mostly yelp comments), since the food is good
#Had an old batch of the Braised Pork Belly (Hong Sau Rou).  I will give them another chance.  This is an easy dish.
#The dry onion oil noodles 葱油拌面 were good.  Yelpers just don’t know about this noodle dish.
