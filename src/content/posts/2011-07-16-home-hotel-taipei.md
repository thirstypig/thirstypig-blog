Located in the heart of the XinYi district, Home Hotel is the latest trend of new hotels to pop up, with the Le Meridian, the W, and Tango opening recently. Home Hotel is an upscale boutique hotel with the service and comfort of a Bed & Breakfast.

What sets Home Hotel apart is the great location with exciting shops, venues, and establishments just a short walk away. With Taipei 101, Mitsukoshi Shopping Center, Warner Village, and countess restaurants, its as if you are in Tokyo’s Ginza district, Hong Kong’s Central District, or Seoul’s Myeongdong area – all rolled into one. The hottest clubs and lounges – Sparkx, Marquee,Room 18, Bar Code, Lava, In House, and Brown Sugar are just a hop, skip, and a jump away.

As you walking in first floor boutique you notice the dazzling array for goods designed and produced locally by Taiwanese artisans. Some of the brands include Zenique teas and Brut Cake designs.

The third floor lobby features a minimalist design and layout. The centerpiece seating piece, was created just for Home Hotel, which emulates a chair as well as a sofa. Just beyond the Study Room, meeting & business space, is the Marsalis Home x Whisky Gallery.

Each of the anointed rooms, the Original, Marvelous, and Extraordinary, feature wood tones, soft gray granite, and stainless steel, which is seen repeated throughout the hotel. The furnitures is from Strauss with detailed amenities are from MRKT, STY, and Brut Cake.

You get a feeling that this hotel isn’t your ordinary run of the mill one, but one with the coziness and comfort of bed and breakfast, yet with style of a boutique hotel.
