#Very good Ox Tail Soup, Sul Lung Tang (Beef), and Galbi Tang (Beef Rib) soups
#Seems like the only Korean restaurant in the area
#Hours are a bit strange, since they don’t actually serve at dinner time, closes at 6pm, and closes at 2pm on Saturday.
#But they do open somewhat early for breakfast at 6:30am
