#Solid Vietnamese Noodle restaurant featuring Pho
#RIP Fred. Glad I got to meet him, unfortunate that he passed away so young
#Personal favorite is the beef tomato broth with dipping french bread – Bo Kho with Baguette
#Broth is not too heavy. I think it is how Chinese like it. My Wife loves it
#There is a small parking lot at a school across from the restaurant where you can park
