Favorite Shanghainese Restaurants
1. [Old Jesse 吉士, Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2010/12/22/restaurant-jesse-吉士-of-shanghai/) – the steamed fish head and drunken crab
2. [小白桦酒家 XiaoBaiHua, Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2011/11/25/小白桦酒家-xiaobaihua-shanghai/) – deep fried frog legs
3. [Restaurant Art Salon, Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2011/08/31/restaurant-art-salon-shanghai/) – crab roe
4. [Fu 1088 福一零八八, Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2010/10/28/fu-1088-福一零八八/) – braised pork belly
5. [夏味馆 Xia Restaurant, Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2010/09/23/sweating-at-夏味馆-xia-restaurant-of-shanghai/) – the braised crab
Honorable Mention. [HaiJinZi海金滋添加分店 , Shanghai](http://www.thethirstypig.com/2011/10/28/hai-jinzi-shanghai/) – the egg dish
