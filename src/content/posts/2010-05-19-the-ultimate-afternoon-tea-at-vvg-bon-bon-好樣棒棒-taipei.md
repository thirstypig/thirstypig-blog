VVG Bon Bon is a quaint little afternoon tea place. It is so cute and feminine that I had no business being there.

The “Alice in Wonderland” influenced shop is ideal for desserts, tea, and little snacks. Part of the VVG area chain, which includes a hotel, restaurant, and tea shop, Bon Bon rounds this group with its unique style.

Their menu includes an assortment of teas and drinks, yet I choose the Cherry Soda with Balsamic Vinegar. The presentation has a small carafe of vinegar,

What to Order: everything!
What not to Order:
