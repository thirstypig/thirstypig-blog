Marsalis, located in the 3rd floor lobby of the Home Hotel, is a Jazz/Whiskey Lounge. The combination of Jazz and Whiskey offers a refiner experience for the discerning.

With dozens of different whiskeys and scotches, Marsalis overwhelmed me with their fine selections. I had previously thought I had a decent knowledge of single malts and blended ones, but Marsalis takes it to another level. As part of [The Scotch Malt Whiskey Society](http://www.smws.co.uk/), Marsalis features a large selection of their exclusive collection. You’ll notice a special shelf with unique label denoting numbers that correspond to distilleries and age – all single malts!

Live Jazz is also featured at Marsalis. I have always said that live music is best listened to in small concert venues. A small 4 piece group would do nicely at Marsalis.

What could be better – some smooth Jazz and a great glass of scotch, both of which you’ll find at Marsalis.
