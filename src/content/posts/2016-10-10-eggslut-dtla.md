#Get there early or arrive around 3pm in the afternoon on the weekdays to avoid the long lines
#The Eggslut is the coddled egg, which is pretty good, but you have to let it cool down a bit
#The egg sandwiches are all great, except for the steak one (the meat with the egg is a little more messy)
#Lots of seating in and around the counter and throughout Grand Central Market
#Parking is a pain, just bite the bullet and pay for parking in the structure next door.

#### Eggslut Grand Central Market 317 S Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90013 (213) 625-0292 Hours – 8AM–4PM
