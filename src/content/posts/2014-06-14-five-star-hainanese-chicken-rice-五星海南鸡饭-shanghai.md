#Solid Hainan chicken. I like it. Might be the best in Shanghai (I am not Singaporean, so I am not an expert)
#Gets crowded fast. Queue form up quickly at lunchtime
#There is a good parking lot about a 5 min walk away
#Chicken is moist and not dry (a given)
#A single chicken order is enough for 2 people
