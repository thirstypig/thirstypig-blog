Nectar of gods or motor oil.  Moutai Jiu is undisputed and most popular white wine of China.  My classmates and I had a great dinner with Moutai.  From the southwest region of China in Guizhou, Moutai has a unique flavor - you have to grow to like it.  But some people will feel that Chinese white wine is like motor oil or rubbing alcohol.  Strong AF.  I do not recommend going up against anyone in a drinking contest if they can drink Chinese white wine.  The typical shot of white wine is the size of a thimble.  When u was in HKG I had a few drinking buddies who called ourselves the Moutai club. I have loads of stories of Chinese white wine. Another unique thing about this stuff is- you won’t get a hangover.  I also think that Moutai, like tequila, get better after the third shot.  Sorry for the ramblings.
